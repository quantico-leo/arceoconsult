About Us

ArceoConsult is a Nordic boutique focused on restructuring and founder protection. We work alongside law firms, lenders, and advisors to safeguard owner interests, restore governance, and give clear direction in uncertain times.

Services

Cash clarity

A simple 13-week cash plan that shows how long funds last — trusted by lenders and giving you peace of mind.

Creditor dialogue

We prepare respectful standstill requests and lead the dialogue with banks and suppliers — reducing pressure and buying you time.

Founder protection

We secure governance, access control, and a practical rhythm so you as the founder keep control of the company.

Cross-border structuring

For international operations we coordinate with local counsel in the EU and UK for entity resets, exits, and asset shielding.

Your Advantage

You gain time, stability, and negotiation strength. With clear cash planning and structured creditor dialogue, noise goes down, trust goes up, and you can lead with confidence instead of reacting in stress.
